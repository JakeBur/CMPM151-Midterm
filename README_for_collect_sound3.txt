To hear any sound at all, you must first turn on the dsp bby clicking on the box on
the top left corner. Then make sure that the volume is at least 1 or higher.
collect_sound3.pd will take an integer ranging from 0 to 4 from "r osccollect" object. 
That integer will determine which sound will be selected and played when the player 
collects a collectable. The volume of the sound can be changed and adjusted by moving
the slider up (louder) and down (quieter). To change the pitch of the sounds, you can
also changed the the 5 numbers objects that are beneath/connected to the selector object
as well as the number connected to the loadbang object.
